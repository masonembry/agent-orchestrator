# MVP Scope

## 1. Data Science Scope

### Inputs

- **Real-time call transcript only**
    - Rationale: GAIA does not have access to historical call transcripts. Incorporating historical transcripts would be net new. Depending on desired timeline for pilot, likely not feasible.
    - Signals derived from transcript:
        - Tone / emotional cues
        - Customer attributes (e.g., tech savviness, presence of children, number/type of devices, etc.)
    - Excluded from MVP: Claims data, prior call history, C360, historical interaction data

### Outputs

- **Tone Guidance** *(Mirror Field approach; reuse if possible)*
    - Provide a concise recommendation for how the expert should frame the offer.
    - Example: “Empathize with busy family life. Focus on simplicity and future-proofing their growing tech needs.”
- **Three Pitch Points** *(Mirror Field approach; reuse if possible)*
    - Grounded in a pool of pre-approved, marketing-written pitch points, allowing light contextual tailoring (no fully new pitch creation)
    - Ranked dynamically based on transcript signals.
    - Examples: “As your kids grow, VHDP auto-covers new eligible tablets or smart speakers — no need to add them later” ; “If ADH occurs on your laptop, we’ll repair, replace, or reimburse for covered breakdowns” ; “One plan protects your tablet, laptop, and eligible future tech — no juggling multiple plans”

## 2. Workspace/Alpha Experience (How It Comes Together)

### What Experts See

Workspace/Alpha combine Data Science generated outputs with existing Smart Offer components to create a structured offer framework with 2 parts:

1. **Tone Guidance** *(DS output)*
2. **Offer Scripting**
    1. Transition statement *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model
    2. Discovery question *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model
    3. Engagement question *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model
    4. Three pitch points *(DS output) (Note: This output will replace the section in the Smart Offer that is currently referred to as “Core Benefits”)* - No detection models for MVP
        - Where is “Example of Coverage” from Smart Offer?
            
            What we currently refer to as the “Example of Coverage” is intended to personalize coverage details based on the devices the customer owns. However, this intent is already fulfilled by switching from static plan benefits to the three generated pitch points, which tailors plan benefits using known customer information (e.g., their devices). For MVP, we will remove the current “Example of Coverage” to avoid redundancy. 
            
    5. Price *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model
    6. Ask for sale *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model
    7. Ask to send link *(Static, reuse existing Smart Offer language) -* Maintain existing detection model

### Content Generation & Refresh Logic

**Initial Generation (Plan Card Click)**

- Content generation is triggered when the expert enters the offer flow (Clicks the plan card to open the sales guided flow for Workspace, enters the sales opportunity page for Alpha)
- If sufficient transcript data is available → Generate Tone Guidance + Three Pitch Points.
- If sufficient transcript data is not available → Do not surface Tone Guidance (show placeholder in UI) and display the current three static Smart Offer plan benefits in place of the generated Three Pitch Points.

**Once Inside the Offer Flow**

- To preserve stability and avoid distraction, no automatic refresh will occur once the offer is visible.
- Provide a manual “Refresh” option if the expert wants updated guidance or if they opened the offer flow before sufficient data was available for content generation.

**Exit & Re-Entry Behavior**

- If the expert exits the offer flow, content will regenerate on next entry (Clicks the plan card to open the sales guided flow for Workspace, enters the sales opportunity page for Alpha)

- How might we measure expert usage of Tone Guidance and Pitch Points in order to attribute impact?
    1. Data Science: Retroactively compare full transcript to generated smart pitch to identify if being used & at what rate
        1. Batch of 400-500 calls
    2. Can also consider leveraging the Quality team to conduct targeted manual reviews.
- Introduce real-time detection models expert mention of generated content
- Expanded content generation
    - Contextual transition statements
        - Personalized bridges into the offer based on issue resolution
    - Dynamic discovery questions
        - Tailored to inferred customer context (e.g., household size, tech complexity, prior pain points)
    - Dynamic engagement questions

    - Provide real-time support once objections are detected in the transcript
